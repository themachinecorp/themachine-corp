Our Story

Founded in 2018, Brew & Bean started as a small cart at the local farmers market. What began as a weekend passion project quickly grew into something more — a community gathering spot where great coffee meets great conversation.

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Every batch is roasted in-house weekly to ensure peak freshness. We believe that exceptional coffee starts with exceptional relationships.
