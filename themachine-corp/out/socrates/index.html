Inside the Experience

What happens in a session

A typical Socrates session takes 10–15 minutes and leaves you with genuine clarity you didn't have before.

01
You state your question — anything you're wrestling with. A career decision. A relationship. A belief you're unsure about.
02
Socrates asks the first probing question. "What do you mean by that?" "What's your evidence?" "What would have to be true for this to be false?"
03
You answer. Socrates follows up. The AI tracks your reasoning and asks increasingly precise questions, typically 3–5 rounds.
04
You get your thinking report. A structured summary of what you said, what was revealed, and what conclusions (if any) you reached.
